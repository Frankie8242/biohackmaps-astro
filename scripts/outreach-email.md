# Venue Owner Outreach Email Templates

Use these to email venue owners and drive claims + backlinks.

---

## Template 1: "You're Listed" (cold outreach)

**Subject:** Your venue is featured on BiohackMap

Hi {owner_name},

I wanted to let you know that {venue_name} is listed on BiohackMap — the world's largest directory of biohacking venues.

Your listing currently shows:
- Rating: {rating} from {review_count} reviews
- Modalities: {modalities}
- City: {city}

You can see your page here: https://biohackmaps.com/venues/{slug}

We get thousands of monthly visitors searching for {modality} venues in {city}. You can claim your listing for free to:

- Update your hours, services, and contact info
- Ensure your details are accurate
- Rank higher in our search results

Claim your free listing here: https://biohackmaps.com/claim?venue={venue_name_encoded}

If you'd like to rank #1 in {city} searches and get a gold verified badge, check out our Premium plan ($49/mo): https://biohackmaps.com/for-business

Best,
BiohackMap Team
support@biohackmap.com

- Rank #1 in all {city} searches

- Receive monthly analytics on how many people view your listing

See your current listing: https://biohackmaps.com/venues/{slug}

Interested? Reply to this email or upgrade here: https://biohackmaps.com/for-business

Best,
BiohackMap Team

## Template 3: "Link Request" (after they claim)

**Subject:** Thanks for claiming — one quick favour?

Hi {owner_name},

Thanks for claiming your {venue_name} listing on BiohackMap!

Quick question — would you be open to adding a small link to your BiohackMap page from your website? Something like:

"Find us on BiohackMap" linking to: https://biohackmaps.com/venues/{slug}

This helps more people discover your venue through our directory, and it helps us rank higher in Google so we can send you more visitors. Win-win.

No pressure at all — just thought I'd ask.

Best,
BiohackMap Team

---

## Sending Strategy

1. **Week 1:** Send Template 1 to all venues with email addresses in the database
2. **Week 2:** Follow up with non-responders (shorter version: "Just checking you saw this")
3. **Week 3:** Send Template 2 to top-rated venues (4.9+ with 100+ reviews)
4. **Ongoing:** Send Template 3 to every venue that claims their listing

## Finding Emails

Many venues have email in the database already. For others:
- Check the venue's website contact page
- Use the venue's Google Maps listing
- Check their social media bios

## Tracking

Track in a spreadsheet:
- Venue name | Email | Date sent | Template used | Claimed? | Premium? | Backlink?
